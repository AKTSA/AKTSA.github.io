AK TSA

Innovate Today. Change Tomorrow.

We are the Ardery Kell High School Technology Student Association, a school supported club that participates in several technology-based events and projects. AK TSA also competes against other school's TSA clubs at the Regional, State, and National level!

Student Projects

Fashion Design

The Technology Student Association at Ardrey Kell creates and designs new trends in fashion..

VEX Robotics

The Technology Student Association at Ardrey Kell builds robots to perform specific tasks.

Campus Cleanup

The Technology Student Association at Ardrey Kell is always helping the school out. One of the many ways we do this is with a Campus Clean up.

Animatronics

The Technology Student Association at Ardrey Kell makes robot bunnies that help teach kids how to brush their teeth in a child friendy way.

2nd Harvest Food Bank

The Technology Student Association at Ardrey Kell participates in helping the Second Harvest Food Bank collect money.

Webmasters
