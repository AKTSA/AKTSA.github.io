Innovate Today. Change Tomorrow.
Our motto is a reflection of what we believe to be not only true, but possible. In our modern, interconnected world, technology literacy is more important than ever. The beauty of technology is that it allows anyone, no matter their age, gender, race, ethnicity, or religion to take a part in the change they wish to see in the world. Technology empowers everyone to do great things. And that's exactly what we aim to do.

The official crest of AK TSA represents the spirit of our competitors. Its abnormal shape and design symbolizes our uniqueness and the innovative and modern perspective of the club, while the structure of the Rubik's Cube reminds us that we are all part of a greater whole, working together to better the world.
