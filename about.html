Community Service Activities

Campus Cleanup

As part of a coordinated effort across all clubs in the school to beautify the campus and create an environment more condusive to education, the AK TSA maintains a sector of the school, cleaning the area and keeping it presentable for the students to enjoy.

Food Bank

Loaves and Fishes is a local charitable organization which distributes donated food to people in need via local food pantries. Students from AK TSA help obtain cans, collect donations, and sort food to help the entire process.

Cancer Cure Bracelets

To help raise awareness of and fund research for a cure to cancer, students from AK TSA sold wristbands to students across the school. 100% of proceeds from the sales were donated to charity to help find "AKure".
